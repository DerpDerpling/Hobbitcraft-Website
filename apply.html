Make your application detailed!
ALL PLAYERS MUST FILL OUT AN APPLICATION FOR THE SERVER UNLESS TOLD OTHERWISE BY STAFF. ANY MEMBER YOU REFER TO THE SERVER WILL REWARD THE REFERAL WITH A POINT ON DISCORD. Please dont make joke applications


Discord Rules

(1) Be respectful and don’t be toxic. We want this to be a pleasant community.

(2) Racial and Homophobic language WILL NOT be tolerated. Jokes about mental health, suicide, bullying and similar should be made tastefully, if at all.

(3) Listen to the mods at all times, especially if they are trying to enforce a rule. If you believe that an Admin is abusing their power, report it to another Admin or the Owner.

(4) Please do NOT spam. This includes chat and voice chat ("ear rape") spam. Please refrain from writing the same message repeatedly or sending copypastas.

(5) Please do not start any drama or arguments with anyone in the server. If you really wish to argue with someone, bring it to DM's and keep it out of the server.

(6) Do NOT attempt to find anyone's personal information, such as names, addresses, phone numbers, etc., Do not DOX yourself, or others, any weird links are not tolerated.

(7) Do not harass admins and demand that they look at your whitelist application. We have lives and will not always be able to read over them. There is a schedule for applications.

MC Rules

(1) Do not grief or steal. Taking items from a chest you don’t own or breaking a building you didn’t build is illegal, unless the owner of the chest/build agrees to it. This includes farms: using someone’s farm without their permission is not allowed. As long as they are done in good taste, pranking someone’s base/build is allowed, but the cleanup process of the prank should definitely take less than an hour.

(2) Pvp is not illegal, but it’s only accepted in moderation. Killing someone continuously without their consent is illegal. Unless agreed upon differently by the involved parties, all items dropped need to be returned to their owner (this does not include the head drop that happens when a player kills a player). If a player attacks you and you kill them in self defense that’s also allowed.

(2.1) In case the victim loses their items because of how the attacker killed them (e.x. multiple explosions or lava) the attacker has to use his own resources to get back/farm back the victim's item. This does not jump into effect if the attacker loses its items because the victim used self defense. This is a risk the attacker needs to take.

(3) Do not xray or cheat. Please do not use hacked/modded clients that give you an advantage over other players. Using tnt dupers, bedrock breakers or similar exploits is accepted however. Straight up ITEM DUPERS ARE NOT ALLOWED (ex. Exploits that can duplicate shulkers of items).

(3.1) The clay patch trick counts as Xraying/Cheating, using it will result in the same punishment as Xraying
